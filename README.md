# Cybersecurity AI Cerebral

The Cybersecurity AI Cerebral is Active Circuitry’s advanced AI model built to operate in real time. Its job is to continuously scan and interpret near to 100,000 signals every week, identifying cyberattack patterns as they emerge. This constant flow of data allows the Cybersecurity AI Cerebral to adapt quickly to new tactics, spotting subtle anomalies that traditional defenses might miss.

What makes it especially powerful is how it works in tandem with ActiveGuardNet, Active Circuitry's Forward Deployed Cybersecurity AI Model. ActiveGuardNet provides the offline resilience, protecting systems even when connectivity is limited while Cerebral adds the online intelligence, processing live signals and updating defenses dynamically. Together, they form a dual-layer security system: one that is always available and one that is always adaptive.

In practice, this means organizations and individuals benefit from a shield that doesn’t just react to attacks but anticipates them. ActiveGuardNet ensures protection in isolated environments, while Cerebral ensures that once connected, systems are plugged into a vast, continuously updated intelligence network capable of recognizing malicious behavior with precision.
